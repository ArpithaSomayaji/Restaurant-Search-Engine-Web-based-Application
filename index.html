About us

“Laughter is brightest, where food is best”. No one wants to cook all the time. Some days, you just want to kick back and relax by heading out to a delicious establishment near you. May it be your first visit to a new place or be it an occasion worth making it memorable, you would want to select the best restaurant for yourself. How do you know where the best places to dine are? Sure, you can ask your friends or simply take the plunge and try somewhere new, but sometimes you could do with more focused help. This is where Restaurant Search Engine can help you in locating list of restaurants based on the location, cuisine of your choice, reviews and feedback and convenience. It keeps track of Customer Ratings where you can follow people whose tastes you trust so their tips show up, along with advice on what to order. The application also provides the information like Restaurant details, Budget, various cuisines offered by the restaurants. The customer can also make a reservation under any restaurant depending on the availability. The application allows you to compare the restaurant choices by rating, distance, cuisines and popularity levels, which is very helpful especially when you are new to city. You can browse photos of the food menus, as well as look at online restaurant guides by critics as well as other food fans.
